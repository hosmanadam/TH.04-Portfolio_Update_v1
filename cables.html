Cables

Making audio cables is also something I enjoy. It started as a mere necessity, because I needed an elaborate cable and had nobody to make it for me.

However, I found a sense of fulfillment in the process that I was previously lacking in music, so I made more and more, eventually for friends and then other customers.

There's just something special about having four things on your left, going through a well-defined 5-minute process of assembling and testing it, then putting it down on your right and knowing that it's done.

Try to do that with music!
